# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 11, 2026

## What We Collect

**Account Information:**
- Email address (for magic link authentication)
- Name and optional affiliation (if you create a participant profile)

**Contributions:**
- Text content you submit
- Metadata (timestamps, participant ID)
- AI-extracted artifacts and classifications

**Technical Data:**
- Anonymous usage analytics (page views, feature usage)
- Error logs for debugging

## How We Use Your Data

- **Authentication:** Email addresses are used solely for magic link sign-in
- **Knowledge Graph:** Contributions become part of the public knowledge graph
- **Platform Improvement:** Analytics help us improve the user experience
- **No Advertising:** We do not sell data or use it for advertising

## Data Sharing

- **Public by Default:** Contributions and participant profiles are publicly visible
- **No Third-Party Sales:** We do not sell or rent your data
- **Service Providers:** Supabase (database/auth) and Anthropic (AI extraction) process data under strict agreements

## Your Rights

- **Access:** View your contributions at any time
- **Deletion:** Contact hello@commons.id to request account deletion
- **Portability:** Export your data via JSON-LD export feature

## Data Retention

- Active contributions: Retained indefinitely as part of the knowledge graph
- Deleted accounts: Email removed immediately; contributions may remain attributed to "Anonymous"

## Security

- Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest (AES-256)
- Magic link authentication (no passwords to compromise)
- Regular security audits and backups

## Changes

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be announced via the platform.

## Contact

Questions or concerns: hello@commons.id
