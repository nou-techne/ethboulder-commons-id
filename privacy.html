Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

1. What We Collect

Account information: Email address (used for magic link authentication). Name and affiliation (if you choose to provide them).

Contributions: Observations, session notes, and other content you submit to convergence events.

Usage data: Basic analytics including page views and feature usage, collected to improve the platform.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate you and provide access to the platform
  • To display your contributions as part of the knowledge commons
  • To improve the platform experience
  • To communicate with you about your account or contributions

3. What We Share

We do not sell your personal data. Contributions you make to convergence events are part of a shared knowledge commons and are visible to other participants. We may share data with:

  • Supabase: Our database and authentication provider
  • Anthropic: Contributions are processed by Claude AI to extract structured knowledge. The content of your contributions is sent to Anthropic's API for processing.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure. We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us. You may delete your account at any time.

6. Cookies

commons.id uses local storage for authentication tokens and theme preferences. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

7. Open Source Transparency

The entire commons.id platform is open source. You can inspect exactly how your data is handled by reviewing the code.

8. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at the project repository.

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
